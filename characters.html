Mahabharata Characters

Yudhishthira

Role

Eldest Pandava; King of Indraprastha and Hastinapura

Family

Son of Kunti and Dharma (Yama)

Notable Deeds

Embodiment of dharma; lost in the dice game, accepted exile; led the Pandavas to victory and ascended the throne.

Key Events

  • Circa 5580 BCE: Born to Kunti via Yama.
  • Circa 5562 BCE: Lost kingdom in the dice game.
  • Circa 5561 BCE: Crowned king of Hastinapura after the war.

Bhima

Role

Second Pandava; Mace-Warrior

Family

Son of Kunti and Vayu (Wind God)

Notable Deeds

Physical powerhouse; avenged Draupadi by killing Dushasana; defeated Duryodhana.

Key Events

  • Circa 5580 BCE: Born to Kunti via Vayu.
  • Circa 5562 BCE: Exiled with Pandavas after the dice game.
  • 2 Nov 5561 BCE: Defeated Duryodhana in a mace fight.

Arjuna

Role

Third Pandava; Greatest Archer

Family

Son of Kunti and Indra

Notable Deeds

Master archer; key figure in Kurukshetra; recipient of the Bhagavad Gita; slew Bhishma, Karna, Jayadratha.

Key Events

  • Circa 5563 BCE: Won Draupadi at her swayamvara.
  • 16 Oct 5561 BCE: Received the Bhagavad Gita from Krishna.
  • 1 Nov 5561 BCE: Killed Karna in battle.

Nakula & Sahadeva

Role

Fourth and Fifth Pandavas

Family

Sons of Madri and the Ashvins

Notable Deeds

Nakula – horse-keeper and swordsman; Sahadeva – wisdom, astrologer; Sahadeva killed Shakuni.

Key Events

  • Circa 5580 BCE: Born to Madri via the Ashvins.
  • Circa 5562–5549 BCE: Exiled with Pandavas.
  • 2 Nov 5561 BCE: Sahadeva killed Shakuni during the war.

Draupadi

Role

Queen of the Pandavas

Family

Daughter of King Drupada

Notable Deeds

Central to all key events; first to enter Pandavas in exile; humiliated in dice game; her insult spurred Bhima’s vow.

Key Events

  • Circa 5563 BCE: Married to the Pandavas after Arjuna’s victory.
  • Circa 5562 BCE: Humiliated in the Kaurava court.
  • Circa 5562–5549 BCE: Exiled with the Pandavas.

Krishna

Role

Incarnation of Vishnu; Strategist and Charioteer

Family

Son of Vasudeva and Devaki

Notable Deeds

Diplomat, guide; delivered Bhagavad Gita; pivotal in ensuring Pandava victory.

Key Events

  • 16 Oct 5561 BCE: Delivered the Bhagavad Gita to Arjuna.
  • 29 Oct 5561 BCE: Helped Arjuna kill Jayadratha with an illusion.
  • Post-5561 BCE: Advised Yudhishthira on governance.

Karna

Role

Greatest Rival to Arjuna; Ally of Duryodhana

Family

Son of Kunti and Surya; raised by Adhiratha

Notable Deeds

Master archer, rival to Arjuna; defeated Jarasandha; killed by Arjuna on Day 17.

Key Events

  • Circa 5580 BCE: Born to Kunti via Surya, abandoned at birth.
  • 29 Oct 5561 BCE: Killed Ghatotkacha with the Shakti weapon.
  • 1 Nov 5561 BCE: Killed by Arjuna during the war.

Bhishma

Role

Elder Statesman; Commander of the Kauravas

Family

Son of Shantanu and Ganga

Notable Deeds

Led Kaurava forces for 10 days; fell to Arjuna via Shikhandi; lay on arrow-bed until Uttarayana.

Key Events

  • Circa 5600 BCE: Raised Pandu and Dhritarashtra.
  • 16 Oct 5561 BCE: Led Kauravas at the war’s start.
  • 25 Oct 5561 BCE: Fell to Arjuna and Shikhandi.

Drona

Role

Royal Guru; Kaurava General after Bhishma

Family

Son of Bharadwaja; father of Ashwatthama

Notable Deeds

Formed Chakravyuha; killed Dhrishtadyumna; fell after son’s death.

Key Events

  • Circa 5565 BCE: Trained Pandavas and Kauravas.
  • 28 Oct 5561 BCE: Formed Chakravyuha, leading to Abhimanyu’s death.
  • 30 Oct 5561 BCE: Killed by Dhrishtadyumna.

Duryodhana

Role

Leader of Kauravas; Main Antagonist

Family

Son of Dhritarashtra and Gandhari

Notable Deeds

Orchestrated dice game; led Kauravas into war; died fighting Bhima.

Key Events

  • Circa 5580 BCE: Born to Gandhari.
  • Circa 5562 BCE: Orchestrated the rigged dice game.
  • 2 Nov 5561 BCE: Defeated by Bhima, ending the war.

Shakuni

Role

Kaurava Kingmaker; Gambler-in-Chief

Family

Son of Subala; brother of Gandhari

Notable Deeds

Ruined the dice game; mastermind behind Kaurava misdeeds; slain by Sahadeva.

Key Events

  • Circa 5562 BCE: Rigged the dice game against Yudhishthira.
  • Throughout War: Advised Duryodhana on strategies.
  • 2 Nov 5561 BCE: Killed by Sahadeva.

Vidura

Role

Wise Counselor of Hastinapura

Family

Son of Vyasa and maid Parishrami

Notable Deeds

Forewarned Pandavas; epitome of integrity.

Key Events

  • Circa 5600 BCE: Born to Vyasa and Parishrami.
  • Circa 5562 BCE: Warned Pandavas of the lac house plot.
  • Post-5561 BCE: Continued as a wise counselor.

Drupada

Role

King of Panchala; Draupadi’s Father

Family

Father of Draupadi, Dhrishtadyumna, Shikhandi

Notable Deeds

Rival to Drona; fathered warriors; died in battle.

Key Events

  • Circa 5563 BCE: Hosted Draupadi’s swayamvara.
  • During War: Allied with Pandavas.
  • 5561 BCE: Killed in the Kurukshetra War.

Dhritarashtra

Role

Blind King; Father of the Kauravas

Family

Son of Vyasa and Ambika; married to Gandhari

Notable Deeds

Ruled Hastinapura; fathered the Kauravas; struggled with partiality toward Duryodhana.

Key Events

  • Circa 5600 BCE: Born blind to Ambika.
  • Circa 5580 BCE: Fathered the Kauravas with Gandhari.
  • Post-5561 BCE: Retired to the forest after the war.

Dushasana

Role

Second Kaurava; Duryodhana’s Brother

Family

Son of Dhritarashtra and Gandhari

Notable Deeds

Infamous for dishonoring Draupadi; slain by Bhima.

Key Events

  • Circa 5580 BCE: Born to Gandhari.
  • Circa 5562 BCE: Attempted to disrobe Draupadi in court.
  • During War: Killed by Bhima, fulfilling Bhima’s vow.

Abhimanyu

Role

Arjuna’s Brave Son

Family

Son of Arjuna and Subhadra

Notable Deeds

Died entering Chakravyuha during the war.

Key Events

  • Circa 5544 BCE: Born to Arjuna and Subhadra.
  • 28 Oct 5561 BCE: Entered Chakravyuha but was killed.
  • Post-Death: His death spurred Arjuna’s revenge.

Ashwatthama

Role

Son of Drona; Powerful Warrior

Family

Son of Drona and Kripi

Notable Deeds

Survivor of war; attacked Pandava camp at night.

Key Events

  • Circa 5565 BCE: Trained under Drona.
  • 30 Oct 5561 BCE: His “death” led to Drona’s demise.
  • 2 Nov 5561 BCE: Attacked Pandava camp, killing Draupadi’s sons.

Eklavya

Role

Tribal Prince and Self-Taught Archer

Family

Nishada prince

Notable Deeds

Sacrificed his thumb to Drona to honor him as guru.

Key Events

  • Circa 5565 BCE: Self-taught archery using Drona’s statue.
  • Circa 5565 BCE: Gave his thumb as guru-dakshina.
  • During War: Fought for the Kauravas and was killed.

Gandhari

Role

Dhritarashtra’s Wife; Mother of Kauravas

Family

Daughter of Subala; sister of Shakuni

Notable Deeds

Blindfolded in solidarity; mourned Karna.

Key Events

  • Circa 5600 BCE: Married Dhritarashtra and blindfolded herself.
  • Circa 5580 BCE: Gave birth to 100 sons.
  • Post-War: Retired to the forest with Dhritarashtra.

Ghatotkacha

Role

Bhima’s Son with Demoness

Family

Son of Bhima and Hidimbi

Notable Deeds

Killed by Karna’s Shakti weapon during the war.

Key Events

  • Circa 5570 BCE: Born to Bhima and Hidimbi.
  • During War: Fought fiercely for the Pandavas.
  • 29 Oct 5561 BCE: Killed by Karna’s Shakti weapon.

Shikhandi

Role

Reborn Amba; Warrior

Family

Son of Drupada; sibling of Draupadi

Notable Deeds

Instrumental in Bhishma’s death.

Key Events

  • Reborn as Amba to seek revenge on Bhishma.
  • 25 Oct 5561 BCE: Faced Bhishma, leading to his fall.
  • During War: Fought for the Pandavas.

Dhrishtadyumna

Role

Draupadi’s Brother; Pandava General

Family

Son of Drupada

Notable Deeds

Born to kill Drona; killed Drona during the war.

Key Events

  • Born from a fire sacrifice.
  • During War: Led Pandava forces.
  • 30 Oct 5561 BCE: Killed Drona.
